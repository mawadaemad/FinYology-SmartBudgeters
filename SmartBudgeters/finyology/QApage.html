Frequently Asked Questions

Is your app safe for linking bank accounts?

Yes, we use advanced encryption technologies to protect your data. All communications between our app and the banks are done through secure and encrypted channels. We also do not store any bank login credentials on our servers.

How does the smart financial assistant work?

The smart financial assistant analyzes your spending patterns and compares them with similar users. Based on these analyses, it provides personalized tips to save money, optimize spending, and increase your savings. The assistant learns from your habits over time to give more accurate suggestions.

What is the cost of using the app?

The app is free to use with basic features. Premium features are available through a monthly subscription. You can use all essential tracking and analysis tools at no cost. Premium access includes advanced reports, a personal financial assistant, and long-term financial planning tools.

How can I export my financial data?

You can export your data anytime in CSV or Excel format. Go to Account Settings and select "Export Data". You can choose the time period and data types to export. The export process takes a few minutes depending on the data volume.

Does the app support multiple currencies?

Yes, the app supports more than 50 global currencies. You can change the default currency from your account settings. The app also automatically converts values when adding transactions in different currencies based on current exchange rates.
